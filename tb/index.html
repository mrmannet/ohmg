Get your .tb.je subdomain for your text based thing


If you're not using tb.je for a text based thing, the admin might cancel your subdomain. If you want to multi-use it for HTTP(S) and your text based thing, please reach out with your subdomain info and your reason. We're generally quite cool, we just don't like spambots ! If you've come here because you found something not cool coming from tb.je, email with the headers or the link to the content for admin review.

What text based things can you create ?


  • a bbs
  • a finger server
  • a gopher server
  • a gemini server
  • a logarion archive (this we're cool with via http(s), no questions)
  • a news (nntp) server
  • ... and anything else you can think of !
